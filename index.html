The AML Data Challenge

Current workflows force analysts to spend most of their time on manual data collection rather than risk assessment and analysis

The Impact on Productivity

Industry research and direct conversations with compliance teams reveal consistent patterns:

1
Core issue

Heavy Operational Load

Analysts spend a significant amount of time retrieving data scattered across disconnected tools, slowing case preparation and delaying critical decisions.

2
Downstream effect

Fragmented Case Context

Key information lives in core banking, monitoring, KYC platforms, internal documents, emails, and spreadsheets. Analysts rebuild context from scratch every time.

3
What teams request

Need for Intelligent Support

Analysts want a partner that centralizes information, prepares context automatically, and reduces manual effort without replacing their judgment.
